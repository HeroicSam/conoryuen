Hotswaps

Following the outbreak of COVID and transition to work from home culture, luxury mechanical keyboards have turned into a rapidly growing industry with a bustling aftermarket. The goal of Hotswaps is to support this growth by bridging the gap between a now huge community and the archaic technology supporting it.
