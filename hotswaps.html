Hotswaps

Following the outbreak of COVID and transition to work from home culture, luxury mechanical keyboards have turned into a rapidly growing industry with a bustling aftermarket. The goal of Hotswaps is to support this growth by bridging the gap between a now huge community and the archaic technology supporting it.

Problem

The keyboard secondary market is made up of a handful of online forums and social media platforms designed for discussion rather than exchange in mind, causing glaring usability problems.

User Empathy

To better understand the fundamental issues affecting keyboard buyers and sellers, our team decided that user interviews would best give a holistic view of the aftermarket landscape. We recruited and interviewed five /r/mechmarket super users to gauge their behaviors and pain points.
