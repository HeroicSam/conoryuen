Hotswaps

Following the outbreak of COVID and transition to work from home culture, luxury mechanical keyboards have turned into a rapidly growing industry with a bustling aftermarket. The goal of Hotswaps is to support this growth by bridging the gap between a now huge community and the archaic technology supporting it.

Problem

Current popular trading platforms come with a host of issues like usability and ecommerce tooling, such as dreadful browsing experiences and the lack of defined transaction systems.

User Empathy

To better understand the fundamentals issues affecting keyboard buyers and seller, our team decided that user interviews would be give a holistic view of the aftermarket landscape. We recruited and interviewed five /r/mechmarket super users to gauge their behaviors and pain points.
