About Me

Hiya, I'm Conor - a designer, tenderfoot web developer, and lifelong student. I love spending time immersing myself in the user's mind and building products that understand the way they think, feel, and behave. Growing up, I've dedicated a lot of energy to observation and figuring out how people interact with their surroundings.

I graduated from The College of New Jersey with a B.S in Finance and a Minor in Chinese Language. During the day, I'm a financial analyst at Nucleus Global. By night, I'm a product designer for an early-stage startup I founded with my friends called Hotswaps (still in development! feel free to visit the site anyway!).
